Guide to Windsor Terrace, Brooklyn

Windsor Terrace is a small-ish neighborhood in Brooklyn, above it is Park Slope and below it is Kennsington. To it's right is prospect park, and Greenwood Cemetary is on it's left. It's known for tree-lined streets, historic architecture, and strong sense of community.

Prospect Park

Prospect park is a fairly large and very beautiufl park, it's west side is along Windsor Terrace. I grew up and still live across the street from it, so I know that there are many different things to do year round.

  • Have a picnic
  • Go on a walk through one of the many trails in the forrest
  • At LeFrac center, you can go ice skating in the winter/fall and roller skating in the summer/spring
  • Go boating on the lake
  • Go running or biking

Le Paddock

Le Paddock is a really nice, delicious, restaurant with French cuisine and brick oven pizza. Everytime I've been the food has been really amazing.


Nitehawk Cinema

Nitehawk Cinema is a really nice movie theater. It serves food as you watch movies, which is where it can get kind of pricy. They show popular movies and indie movies, so theres almost always something good to see.
