Places to go to kill time in Windsor Terrace

Prospect Park

Prospect park is a fairly large and very beautiufl park. There are many things you can do there to occupy your time, here are some I like to do:

  • Have a picnic on one of the feilds
  • Go on a walk through one of the many trails in the forrest
  • At LeFrac center, you can go ice skating in the winter/fall and roller skating in the summer/spring
  • Go boating on the lake
  • Go running or biking

Brooklyn Botanic Garden

Brooklyn Botanic Garden isn't really in Windsor Terrace at all, but it's close by and I went to it a lot growing up so whatever.
